How Lauta Works

Powerful automation, seamlessly integrated.

Lauta runs as a Microsoft Word Add-in—no separate software to install or learn. Open Word, access Lauta from the sidebar, and start generating documents instantly. Our AI engine analyzes your matter data and produces accurate first drafts in seconds.

Behind the scenes, Lauta connects to your matter management system to pull case details, party information, and key dates. Choose from pre-built legal templates or create your own. The AI fills in the blanks, applies proper formatting, and outputs court-ready documents directly into your Word document.
